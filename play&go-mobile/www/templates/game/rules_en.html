Welcome to Trento e Rovereto Play&Go!

It’s easy to participate: track your trips withViaggia Play&Go and choose sustainable mobility solutions to earn points and advance in the game. Have fun collecting points, badges and test yourself with the weekly challenges. Keep an eye on leaderboards: every week you can win prizes and, while having fun, you can contribute making your city more sustainable and smart!

How do I play?

You can track your routes directly in the Home clicking the icons "Walk", "Bike", “Bus”, or “Train“You can also plan, save and track your frequent multimodal trips in the Mobility section. Tracked journeys allow you to earn Green Leaves points in proportion to the km made through sustainable transport (on foot, by bike/bike sharing, public transport or park&ride facilities). Taken trips must be within Trentino area. There are daily limits to trips/Km performed with the various modes (10 Km by walk, 30 Km by bike/bike-sharing, 8 trips with public transport, 4 trips with cableway).
You can win bonus points in Green Leaves:
  • Choosing zero emissions journeys, i.e., exclusively by bike or on foot;
  • Succeeding in your weekly challenges;
  • Inviting your friends to register to the game: your friend, at registration time, needs to provide your player nickname and must be an active player.

Prizes and incentives

During the game you can win weekly and final prizes that reward the top players in the Green Leaves leader boards (weekly and general, respectively).
The game is available through App iPhone, but Apple is not sponsoring in any way this game and does not offer prizes.
At the following link you can download the complete rules of the game: Viaggia Play&Go regulation.

Data protection and privacy

In order to allow the game to take place, it is necessary to collect and process the information relating to planned and saved journeys, to the route of the trips made via the app, to the results of the game. The personal data of the player will be treated in full compliance with the current data protection and privacy legislation, as specified in the document Data processing and privacy Trento and Rovereto Play&Go game
